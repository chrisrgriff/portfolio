Christopher Griffith is an experienced jack of all trades from Wilmington, Delaware with a demonstrated history in SaaS and e-commerce. He offers a unique blend of creative, technical, and analytical skills, and uses design thinking and user centered design principles to solve challenging business problems.

A design thinker

From ideation and problem definition, to testing and evaluation, Christopher's process includes a well crafted approach that both starts and ends with the user in mind.
